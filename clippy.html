Clippy for Chrome

Hackathon:NYU Stupid Hackathon 2017/ Team:Adrienne Ahn, Drew Johnston



Browse the web with good company! Everyone's favorite Word assistant, Clippy, is back and ready to assist you with your browsing experience on Chrome.

About

Clippy Everywhere! is a project that I created for NYU's "Stupid Hackathon" with Adrienne Ahn and Drew Johnston.

Microsoft Word's infamous, adorable, irritating Office Assistant from the 90's, Clippy, is here to help you out every step of the way. This role of this Clippy for Chrome extension encourages you with motivational quotes as you browse the web. Motivational quotes are taken from Halls cough drop wrapper, so you'll never have to tread the vast and mysterious web with unease ever again.
